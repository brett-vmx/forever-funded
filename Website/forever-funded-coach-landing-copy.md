## 11. FAQ

**I've been writing support letters for years. Is this really for me?**
Especially. This isn't a beginner's course — it's a second set of eyes. Even after twenty years, every writer has blind spots in their own work. The Coach catches the ones you can't see, and confirms what you're already doing well.

**Will it change my voice or make my letters sound generic?**
No. The Coach quotes your own words and suggests sharper versions — it never rewrites your letter for you. The goal is to sound *more* like you, not less.

**Is this AI? Can AI really coach me?**
The Coach uses AI to give you thoughtful feedback instantly and privately. But the *judgment* behind it isn't generic AI — it's twenty years of experience, built into every review. Think of it as a first read that's always available, not a replacement for a trusted friend's eyes.

**Is my email private?**
Yes. Your drafts are visible only to you, they move through a completely encrypted pipeline, they're never used to train AI, and you can delete your data anytime.

**What email service does it work with?**
All of them. Because you simply send your draft — a quick test email — to your review address, it works with Mailchimp, Kit, Constant Contact, Gmail, Outlook, whatever you already use. Nothing to install.

**How fast is the report?**
About 83 seconds from the moment you send your draft.

**How does the free trial work?**
You get 10 free credits (a launch bonus for early sign-ups) to try the Coach on real letters. No credit card required to start.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. No contracts, no hoops.
