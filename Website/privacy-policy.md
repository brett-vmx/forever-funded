# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 9, 2026

Forever Funded ("Forever Funded," "we," "us") is operated by VMX Media.
This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.
We wrote it in plain language because our audience deserves that.

## What we collect

- **Your account details.** The email address you sign up with. We use passwordless
  "magic link" sign-in, so we do not store a password.
- **The drafts you send for review.** When you send a supporter email to your
  private review address, we receive and store that draft's content, subject line,
  and sender/recipient metadata so the Coach can review it.
- **The reports we generate.** The coaching report produced for each draft.
- **Basic usage records.** Which reviews you've used, timestamps, and technical
  logs needed to run and secure the service.

## How we use it

- To generate your coaching reports and deliver them to you.
- To operate, secure, and improve the service.
- **To improve the Coach during our early pilot.** So we can make the Coach's
  feedback better, the operator may personally review submitted drafts and the
  reports they produced. This is how we calibrate quality. Your submissions are
  **never** shared with other users, sold, or used to train outside AI models.

## Who can see your data

- **Other users cannot see your data.** Access is enforced at the database level
  (row-level security), so no other user can read your drafts or reports.
- **The operator can access submissions** for the calibration purpose described
  above, and our infrastructure providers process data on our behalf (below).

## Service providers we use

We rely on trusted providers to run the service. Each processes your data only to
provide their service to us:

- **Supabase** — database and authentication.
- **Postmark** — sending and receiving email.
- **Anthropic** — the AI that generates your report, used under commercial terms
  that **do not permit your content to be used to train AI models**.
- **Cloudflare** — hosting and request processing.

## How your data is protected

Your drafts move through an encrypted connection, and data is stored with access
controls that keep users' data separated. No system is perfectly secure, but we
take reasonable measures to protect your information.

## Data retention and deletion

During our early pilot, we retain your drafts and reports so we can review and
improve the Coach. **You can ask us to delete your data at any time** by emailing
hello (at) foreverfunded.org, and we will remove it. We expect to introduce automatic deletion
after a set period as the product matures.

## Your choices

- Request a copy of your data, or its deletion, by emailing hello (at) foreverfunded.org.
- Stop using the service at any time.

## Children

The service is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 16.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we'll post the new effective date here and, for
material changes, let you know.

## Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello (at) foreverfunded.org.
